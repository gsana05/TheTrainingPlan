WEIGHT LIFTING RULES

Bench Press:

1. The lifter must lie on his/her back with shoulders and buttocks in contact with the flat bench surface.

2. The lifter’s shoes must be in contact with the floor. This position shall be maintained throughout the attempt.

3. The head may rise off the bench or move during the performance of the lift.

4. After receiving the bar at arm’s length, the lifter shall lower the bar to his/her chest and await for half a second before pressing.

5. After the half second pause, the bar is pressed upwards to straight arm’s length and held motionless then you "RACK" .

Squat:

1. The lifter shall un-rack the weight and assume an upright position. The bar shall be held horizontally across the shoulders with the hands and fingers gripping the bar and the feet flat on the platform with the knees locked.

2. The lifter must bend the knees and lower the body until the top surface of the legs at the hip joint is lower than the top of the knees
